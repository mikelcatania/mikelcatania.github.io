About

I'm an aerospace engineer with over 13 years of experience in spacecraft flight dynamics at ESA/ESOC. My day-to-day work involves orbit determination, manoeuvre planning, and operations for Earth observation missions — Sentinel-3, Sentinel-6, and the EPS constellation.

In 2025, I took a break to pursue an independent project: the conceptual design of a four-seater general aviation flying wing. That project combined classical aircraft design methods, panel-method aerodynamics, stability analysis, and a custom Python toolchain — and taught me more about honest engineering than any single job has.
